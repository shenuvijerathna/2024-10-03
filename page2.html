Ravana Waterfall

Ravana Falls is a popular sightseeing attraction in Sri Lanka123. It is located in the misty mountains of Ella, Sri Lanka along the road2. The waterfall measures approximately 25 m (82 ft) in height and cascades from an oval-shaped concave rock outcrop1. Ravana Falls is a 3-tier cascading waterfall, creating rock pools along its way down2. The waterfall is named after the demon King Ravana who according to traditional folklore is believed to have ruled Sri Lanka3.
